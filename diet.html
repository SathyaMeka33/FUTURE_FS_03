Pro Tips

Nutrition Tips

Essential habits to supercharge your fitness results

01

Stay Hydrated

Drink 3–4 liters of water daily. Proper hydration boosts metabolism and improves recovery.

02

Avoid Junk Food

Cut processed foods, sugary drinks, and deep-fried snacks. Replace with wholesome alternatives.

03

Eat Every 3–4 Hours

Small, frequent meals keep your metabolism active and prevent overeating at any single meal.

04

Maintain Protein Intake

Aim for 1.6–2.2g of protein per kg of body weight to support muscle growth and repair.

05

Sleep Well

7–8 hours of quality sleep is essential for hormone balance and muscle recovery.

06

Track Your Progress

Keep a food journal or use apps to monitor your intake and make data-driven adjustments.
